What is Elicit?

Elicit is an AI-powered research assistant that helps you find, analyze, and synthesize scientific literature. It's designed to accelerate your research process by automating literature reviews and providing evidence-based insights.

Step-by-Step Guide to Using Elicit

1

Create Your Account

Visit elicit.com and sign up for a free account. You can use your institutional email for better access to research databases.

Account Creation
2

Formulate Your Research Question

Start with a clear, specific research question. Elicit works best with focused queries like "What are the effects of metformin on type 2 diabetes patients?" rather than broad topics.

Research Question
3

Enter Your Query

Type your research question into Elicit's search bar. The AI will automatically find relevant papers and extract key information from abstracts.

Query Input
4

Review and Filter Results

Elicit will present you with relevant papers. Use filters to narrow down by publication date, study type, or methodology. Read the AI-generated summaries to quickly assess relevance.

Filter Results
5

Extract Key Information

Use Elicit's data extraction features to pull out specific information like study populations, interventions, outcomes, and conclusions. This saves hours of manual data collection.

Data Extraction
6

Synthesize Your Findings

Use Elicit's synthesis tools to create summaries, identify patterns across studies, and generate evidence-based conclusions for your research.

Pro Tips for Medical Students

Use Medical Terminology

Include specific medical terms, drug names, and conditions in your queries for more precise results.

Filter by Recency

Medical research evolves quickly. Filter results to the last 5 years for the most current evidence.

Consider Study Types

Specify study types (RCT, meta-analysis, systematic review) to find the highest quality evidence.

Save Important Papers

Use Elicit's bookmarking feature to save relevant papers for your literature review.
